Our Mission

Wellverse Global Awareness is dedicated to making health information accessible to everyone. We believe that by providing clear and helpful health information in many different languages, we can help people from all cultures and backgrounds.

Our goal is to create health information that truly connects with diverse communities, helping them feel empowered to live healthier and happier lives. We're committed to breaking down language and culture barriers so that vital health knowledge is available to all.

We currently share our resources in English, Punjabi, and Hindi, with more languages to come. We believe it's important to meet people where they are, so we actively share our resources within the community. We work with local groups, including senior centers, to ensure our information reaches those who can benefit from it most.

Disclaimer: We use credible sources for our posters. However, we do not give any medical advice and are not licensed doctors. We are just spreading awareness. Always consult with a doctor about diagnoses and medical conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are you licensed doctors?

No, we are not. We are undergraduate students passionate about spreading health awareness in different languages to make reliable information more accessible to everyone.

2. Where do you get your information from?

We use trustworthy sources such as government health agencies, verified organizations, and reputable public health institutions to ensure accuracy and reliability.

3. Are you recruiting?

Not at the moment, but we plan to recruit in the future as our organization grows. Stay connected with us on Instagram for updates!

4. Where are the resources posted?

Our main platform is Instagram, where we share our latest health awareness materials. We also post downloadable resources on our Google Drive.
